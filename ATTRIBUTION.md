# Attribution and Licensing

## Inspiration

The idea that domain experts need their own AI curriculum was inspired by Zhiling Zheng's AI4CHEM course at Washington University in St. Louis.

- **Course**: AI4CHEM: Artificial Intelligence for Experimental Chemistry
- **Citation**: Z. Zheng. *Developing an AI Course for Synthetic Chemistry Students*. arXiv:2511.18244, 2025.

## This Work

*AI for Official Statistics* is an original work written for federal statisticians, survey methodologists, and data professionals in official statistics agencies. The content, examples, pedagogical framing, and all chapters are independent of the AI4CHEM materials.

The State Fidelity Validity (SFV) framework introduced in Chapter 15 is an original contribution by Brock Webb.
